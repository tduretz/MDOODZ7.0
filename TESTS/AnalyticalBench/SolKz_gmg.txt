/**** add-gmg-stokes-defence §3.5 / §4.2 — SolKz high-contrast Stokes (GMG) ****/
/* Zhong & Gurnis SolKz benchmark: analytic Stokes problem with
 * prescribed variable viscosity eta(z) = exp(2·B·z) and sinusoidal
 * body force rho(x,z) = -sin(pi·z)·cos(K·pi·x). Stresses the V-cycle
 * on a non-constant-coefficient operator without plastic complexity —
 * exactly what §4.2 SolKzGmgEquivalence needs to prove GMG is
 * bit-identical to CHOLMOD at 1e-8.
 *
 * MDOODZ has no marker-level density/viscosity callback — the mesh
 * body force and operator come from `materials->rho[p]` and
 * `materials->eta0[p]` (phase tables). We discretise the continuous
 * eta(x,z) · rho(x,z) fields into a 4x4 grid of constant-viscosity
 * phases (one phase per x_band × z_band). The SolKzGmgEquivalence
 * fixture
 *   (1) assigns each marker to phase index (ix + 4*iz) in SetPhase;
 *   (2) overrides rho[p] = -sin(pi·z_mid) cos(K·pi·x_mid) and
 *       eta0[p] = exp(2·B·z_mid) for each of the 16 phases in
 *       MutateInput.
 * The stair-step is consumed identically by CHOLMOD and GMG, so the
 * 1e-8 dual-solver bound is apples-to-apples.                            */
